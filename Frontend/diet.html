Pneumonia:

Key Nutrients

  • Fluids
  • Protein (moderate)
  • Vitamins C & A
  • Warm foods

Foods to Avoid

  • Fried and greasy foods
  • Cold drinks
  • Very spicy food
  • Sugary sodas

Remember

  • Light, warm diet
  • Focus on hydration
  • Short-term recovery
  • Moderate protein

Tubercluosis(TB):

Key Nutrients

  • High protein (eggs, meat, dairy, beans)
  • High calories
  • Vitamins A, C, D
  • Iron & zinc

Foods to Avoid

  • Junk food
  • Alcohol & smoking (very harmful in TB)
  • Sugary drinks

Remember

  • High-calorie diet
  • Focus on weight gain
  • Long-term nutrition
  • High protein
